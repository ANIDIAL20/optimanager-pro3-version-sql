# **App Name**: OptiManager Pro

## Core Features:

- Client Management: Create, read, update, and delete client profiles with contact details and insurance information. Implements CRUD operations.
- Prescription Tracking: Log and manage client prescriptions, including lens specifications and prescription dates.
- Product Catalog: Maintain a comprehensive catalog of eyewear products with details like SKU, name, price, and stock level.
- Sales and Order Management: Process sales orders, track payment status, and calculate outstanding balances.
- Inventory Management: Monitor and update stock levels based on sales and restocks, with automated alerts for low stock.
- Payment Processing: Record and manage payments, integrating with various payment methods.
- Inventory Forecasting Tool: Utilize AI to analyze sales trends and predict future inventory needs, optimizing stock levels.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1A237E) to convey professionalism and trust, nodding to the seriousness of healthcare.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) to ensure readability and a clean interface.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) to highlight interactive elements, and call out the technical and forward-looking aspects of eye care. (Avoid heavy use to make it a visual accent only.)
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for clear, readable text throughout the application.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif) for a touch of elegance and classic appeal.
- Use a consistent set of icons to represent actions and categories within the application. Icons should be modern and minimalistic.
- Employ a responsive layout that adapts to different screen sizes. Prioritize ease of navigation.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience, without distracting from functionality.
